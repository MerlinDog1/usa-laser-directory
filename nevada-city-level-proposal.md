## Notes

1. **Distributor Coverage:** Nevada is served by out-of-state distributors (Arizona and California). No major laser distributor is physically based in Nevada.

2. **Las Vegas Dominance:** Most quality engravers and sign companies are concentrated in Las Vegas metro (15 of 22 candidates).

3. **Reno/Sparks Gap:** Reno has fewer dedicated laser engravers but has sign company presence.

4. **Contact Completeness:** Many businesses lack easily discoverable email addresses. LV Laser Engraving and 3 Point Precision are best for contact completeness.

5. **Firearm Niche:** Several Las Vegas engravers specialize in firearm engraving (LV Laser Engraving, BVW Jewelers).
