## Distributor Analysis

Found 2 key distributors serving Colorado:

1. **Innovative Cutting Systems** (Gilbert, AZ) - Epilog, TYKMA, KERN, BOFA, Purex - serves CO as part of multi-state region
2. **Epilog Laser Corp** (Golden, CO) - Headquarters manufacturer, also direct distribution

**Note:** Colorado has limited in-state laser equipment distributors. Epilog is headquartered in Golden but primarily manufactures - their distribution is handled through regional partners like Innovative Cutting Systems (AZ-based).

## Observations

1. **Northern Colorado** (Fort Collins, Loveland, Greeley) has strong engraver presence
2. **Denver Metro** dominates sign companies and full-service engravers
3. **Colorado Springs** has multiple small engravers but limited contact data
4. **Distributor gap** - few in-state distributors; mostly served by AZ-based or manufacturer-direct
5. **Golden** is notable as Epilog Laser headquarters (major laser manufacturer)

---

## Recommendation

This proposal recommends moving forward with the 11 merge-ready entries, prioritizing:

1. **Artcraft Sign Company** - strongest sign company
2. **Boulder Works** - best contact info for engraver
3. **Mile High Laser Engraving** - most comprehensive services
4. **Innovative Cutting Systems** - key distributor relationship

Secondary priority for enrichment: Engraving Colorado (Aurora), Synergy Laser (Colorado Springs), and Engraved. (Colorado Springs) - these have websites but need email/phone verification.
