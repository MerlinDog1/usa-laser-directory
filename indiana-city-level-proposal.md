## Notes

1. **Distributor strength is unusually clear:** Indiana has a strong in-state distributor lead via Fairway Laser Systems in Valparaiso. It already appears to anchor neighboring Midwest states and should also be treated as a primary Indiana distributor entry.

2. **Indianapolis is sign-heavy:** Indianapolis produced multiple credible sign companies with stronger web presence than engravers. ISF Signs and SignCraft are the strongest larger-format options.

3. **Fort Wayne has the best industrial engraver signal:** Apollo MFG stands out as the most industrially relevant Indiana engraver found in this pass, with stronger manufacturing capability than typical awards/gift shops.

4. **South Bend is solid for contactable local shops:** Turba Custom Engraving and Sign Designs both surfaced usable direct contact info and clear service descriptions.

5. **Some Bloomington/Indianapolis entries need one more pass:** A few candidates appear legitimate, but homepage extraction did not cleanly expose phone or email. They are worth revisiting only if broader geographic density is needed.
