## Quality Scoring Criteria

- **9-10:** Complete contact (email + phone), website active, established business
- **7-8:** Website + at least one contact channel, clear services
- **5-6:** Website exists but limited contact info
- **1-4:** Minimal online presence, avoid unless strategic
